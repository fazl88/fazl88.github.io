About

Hi, I am Fazlur Rahman.

I’m a PhD Researcher in Biotechnology at Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi, India, with a deep passion for cancer biology and translational research. My current research focuses on an integrated multi-omics approach to uncover novel prognostic biomarkers involved in the apoptosis-autophagy crosstalk and to explore therapeutic targets in lung cancer.

By combining genomics, transcriptomics, and proteomics, I aim to better understand the complex molecular networks driving tumor progression and treatment resistance. My work is rooted in the belief that identifying key regulatory mechanisms at the systems level can pave the way for more effective and personalized therapies for lung cancer patients.

In addition to my research, I’m keenly interested in data analysis, bioinformatics, and the application of computational biology tools to solve real-world biomedical challenges. This site is a space to share my work, thoughts, and collaborations as I continue my journey in the life sciences.

Let’s connect and explore opportunities to work together.
